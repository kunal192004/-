Street animals need
our protection. Help
them today.

Every year we rescue more
than 15,000 injured animals
in Nashik, India.

Our mission is to rescue and treat owner-less street animals
of Nashik who have become ill or injured, and through their
rescue inspire the community to care for the lives of all
animals.

Help when it's needed most

Throughout India, animals including dogs, cows, cats and donkeys live on the streets. Most cities don't have hospitals for owner-less animals, which means injured or ill animals often die from treatable conditions. But in Udaipur, a call to our helpline saves a life.
